Marketing Plan

Our search service’s goal is provide users with the most relevant car information based on what they need—ranging from servicing and repairing your own car to advice on how to drive under certain conditions like snow and rain.

Our marketing goals are to attain and maintain a large user base and hopefully catch the eye of some auto parts shops like o’reilly or autozone. Surpass car talk in query results

Our users will be people who aren’t car savvy that own older cars from ages between 16 and up—namely, High school students and community college students (since they can’t live on campus, they have to drive to school on a daily basis)

Our competition would be cartalk.com, and Edmunds.com. They both deal with car information and give advice and car tips and tricks. Cartalk offers car servicing advice as well as advice on how to drive in the winter and the summer. Edmunds offers pricing along with a little bit of car tips and resources. We will be focused more on delivering DIY maintenance, servicing and repair, as well as driving tips -- which Cartalk does. However, we believe we can outdo them in UI/UX design and just having better overall user experience.
